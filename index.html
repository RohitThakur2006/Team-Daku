About us

you can find all the most popular species

Visit our zoo and embark on a captivating journey through a diverse array of habitats where you can encounter a multitude of fascinating animal species from around the world. From majestic big cats prowling in their enclosures to colorful tropical birds soaring overhead, our zoo is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts of all ages. Explore the intricate ecosystems of the rainforest, witness the playful antics of primates swinging through the trees, and marvel at the graceful underwater ballet of our aquatic residents. With educational exhibits, interactive experiences, and conservation initiatives, our zoo offers a unique opportunity to connect with nature and learn about the importance of biodiversity in a fun and engaging way. Come join us and discover the wonders of the animal kingdom right in your own backyard!

stay with pets

At our zoo, we offer a unique opportunity for animal lovers to immerse themselves in a world of furry and feathered friends by staying overnight with our delightful pets. Imagine waking up to the gentle chirping of birds, the playful antics of small mammals, and the soothing presence of our friendly domesticated animals. Whether you fancy cuddling up with a fluffy bunny, taking a leisurely stroll with a charming goat, or simply enjoying the company of our affectionate cats and dogs, our zoo provides a cozy and unforgettable experience for guests seeking a closer connection with our beloved animal companions. Join us for an overnight stay filled with warmth, joy, and the unconditional love of our delightful pets – a truly heartwarming adventure awaits you!
